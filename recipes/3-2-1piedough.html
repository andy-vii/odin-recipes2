Basic Pie Dough (3-2-1)

Description

This basic pie dough will work for any number of different pies, whether sweet or savoury. It's quick and easy to make and it has a wonderful flaky texture once baked.

Ingredients

  • 300g All Purpose Flour
  • 200g Unsalted (or Salted) Butter - Diced
  • 100ml Ice Cold Water
  • 1/4tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Combine the flour, salt, and butter in a mixing bowl
  2. Rub together with your fingertips until approximately large breadcrumb consistency
  3. Add water, bring dough together, shape into a ball, wrap in clingfilm
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before use
  5. Can be stored in the fridge for 3-4 days
